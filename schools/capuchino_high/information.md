# Capuchino High School Information

## General Information
Capuchino High School is located in San Bruno, California, on the San Francisco Peninsula. [1](#ref-1) It is part of the San Mateo Union High School District (SMUHSD). [1](#ref-1) The school is an International Baccalaureate (IB) World School, with the program established in 2004. [1](#ref-1) For the 2025-2026 school year, total enrollment is 1,040 students. [3](#ref-3)

## Mission Statement
"At CHS, we aim for students to feel included as valuable community members who embody the traits of the IB Learner Profile. To this end, we work to create a safe and inclusive learning environment that challenges students to become internationally-minded. In order to do this, we must think, be and act with an openness to the world and recognize our own deep interconnectedness to others." [2](#ref-2)

## Student Demographics (2025-2026)
The ethnic distribution of the student body is as follows: [3](#ref-3)
- **Hispanic/Latinx:** 54%
- **Caucasian:** 16%
- **Asian:** 9%
- **Filipino:** 8%
- **Multi-ethnic:** 7%
- **Pacific Islander:** 5%
- **African American:** <1%

Additional student data for the 2025-2026 school year: [3](#ref-3)
- **Socio-economically disadvantaged:** 37%
- **Speak a language other than English at home:** 50%
- **Multilingual Learners:** 13%
- **Receive Special Education Services:** 12%
