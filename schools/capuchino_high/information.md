## Funding Status
Capuchino High School's funding is governed by the Local Control Funding Formula (LCFF) [2](#ref-2). The school is eligible for federal Title I, Part A allocations, which are reported annually by the California Department of Education (CDE) [4](#ref-4). Eligibility is determined by the percentage of low-income and socioeconomically disadvantaged students [2](#ref-2).
