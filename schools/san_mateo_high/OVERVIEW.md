# San Mateo High School Overview

[Back to Schools Overview](../OVERVIEW.md) | [Contacts](contacts.md) | [School Information](information.md)

San Mateo High School (SMHS) is a public high school in San Mateo, California, serving grades 9-12 [4](#ref-4). It is part of the San Mateo Union High School District (SMUHSD) [1](#ref-1).

## Key Information
- **Location:** San Mateo, CA [4](#ref-4)
- **District:** San Mateo Union High School District [1](#ref-1)
