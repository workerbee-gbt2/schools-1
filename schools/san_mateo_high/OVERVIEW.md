# San Mateo High School Overview

San Mateo High School (SMHS) is a public high school in San Mateo, California, serving grades 9-12. It is part of the San Mateo Union High School District (SMUHSD).

## Key Information
- **Location:** San Mateo, CA
- **District:** San Mateo Union High School District
