## General Information
San Mateo High School is a public high school located in San Mateo, California. It is one of seven schools in the San Mateo Union High School District. The school offers a rigorous, standards-based college preparatory program with a 7-period day. [5](#ref-5)

## Mission and Vision Statements
- **Vision:** To equitably prepare all students for a successful future. [6](#ref-6)
- **Mission:** To foster knowledge, growth, and personal responsibility so that our students will make purposeful academic, career, and life choices. [6](#ref-6)
