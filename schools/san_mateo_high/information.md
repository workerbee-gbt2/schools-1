## School Details
- **Location:** 506 North Delaware Street, San Mateo, CA 94401 [1](#ref-1)
- **School District:** San Mateo Union High School District (SMUHSD) [1](#ref-1)
- **Enrollment:** Approximately 1,560 students (Grades 9-12) [1](#ref-1)

## General Information
San Mateo High School is a public high school located in San Mateo, California. It is one of seven schools in the San Mateo Union High School District. The school offers a rigorous, standards-based college preparatory program with a 7-period day. [1](#ref-1)

## Mission and Vision Statements
- **Vision:** To equitably prepare all students for a successful future. [2](#ref-2)
- **Mission:** To foster knowledge, growth, and personal responsibility so that our students will make purposeful academic, career, and life choices. [2](#ref-2)
