# KIPP Valiant Community Prep Information

## Mission
KIPP Valiant's mission is to support students academically, socially, and emotionally to help them grow into passionate, innovative agents of change who lead lives of choice and contribute to a more just world [1](#ref-1). The school's core values are Love, Courage, Justice, Curiosity, and Growth [1](#ref-1).

## School Details
- **Grades Served:** TK-8 [1](#ref-1)
- **Location:** 620 Willow Rd, Menlo Park, CA 94025 [2](#ref-2)
- **Status:** Title 1 (90.8% socioeconomically disadvantaged) [2](#ref-2)
- **Total Enrollment (2024-25):** 587 students [2](#ref-2)
- **Administrator:** Kate Belden [2](#ref-2)

## Student Demographics (2024-25)
- **Socioeconomically Disadvantaged:** 90.8% (533 students eligible for Free/Reduced-Price Meals) [2](#ref-2)
- **English Learners:** 58% (340 students) [2](#ref-2)
- **Primary Language:** Spanish is the predominant non-English language spoken by students [2](#ref-2).
- **Ethnicity:** Approximately 92.7% Hispanic/Latino [3](#ref-3).
