# KIPP Valiant Community Prep Information

[Back to Schools Overview](../OVERVIEW.md) | [View Contacts](contacts.md)

## School Details
- **Location:** 620 Willow Rd, Menlo Park, CA 94025 [1](#ref-1)
- **Status:** Title 1 School [5](#ref-5)
- **Funding Type:** Directly funded charter school [6](#ref-6)
- **Mascot:** Huskies [2](#ref-2)
- **Students:** 575 [7](#ref-7)
- **Demographics:** 92.5% socioeconomically disadvantaged [5](#ref-5). Approximately 90.8% of students are eligible for Free or Reduced-Price Lunch [8](#ref-8). English Language Learners make up 59.7% of the student body [7](#ref-7).
