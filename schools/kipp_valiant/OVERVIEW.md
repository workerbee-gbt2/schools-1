# KIPP Valiant Community Prep Overview

KIPP Valiant Community Prep is a public charter school located in East Palo Alto, CA. It serves students in grades TK-8 and is part of the KIPP (Knowledge Is Power Program) network.

## Summary
The school currently holds a 4/10 rating on GreatSchools, with specific challenges noted in test scores (2/10) and teacher retention based on recent reviews. It offers STEM opportunities through the Project Lead The Way curriculum.
