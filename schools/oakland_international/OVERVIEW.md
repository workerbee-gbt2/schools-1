# Oakland International High School Overview

Oakland International High School (OIHS) is a specialized school in Oakland, CA, serving immigrant and refugee students.

## Athletics
The school has a significant partnership with [Soccer Without Borders](https://www.soccerwithoutborders.org/), providing various soccer teams and coaching staff for students.
