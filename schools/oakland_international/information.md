# Oakland International High School Information

## Mission Statement
The mission of Oakland International High School (OIHS) is to provide a quality alternative education for recently arrived immigrant students in English language acquisition and in preparation for college [1](#ref-1).

## School Details
- **Address:** 4521 Webster Street, Oakland, CA 94609 [1](#ref-1)
- **Phone:** (510) 879-1353 [1](#ref-1)
- **Status:** Title 1 (Verify)
- **Enrollment:** Serves 400+ students annually, with roughly 320 currently enrolled [1](#ref-1) [2](#ref-2).

## Student Demographics
- **Student Profile:** 100% of students are English language learner "newcomers" who have immigrated to the United States within the last four years [2](#ref-2).
- **Countries of Origin:** Students arrive from countries including Guatemala, El Salvador, Honduras, Mexico, Haiti, Brazil, Colombia, China, Vietnam, Afghanistan, Pakistan, Eritrea, Ethiopia, and Yemen [2](#ref-2).
- **Languages Spoken:** Over 35 languages are spoken within the school community [1](#ref-1).
- **Educational Background:** Approximately 30% of students arrive with limited or large gaps in their formal education [2](#ref-2).
