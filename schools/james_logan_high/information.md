## Funding Status
James Logan High School is a Title I school and receives federal Part A funds to support the academic achievement of disadvantaged students [3](#ref-3). Eligibility for Schoolwide Title I programs generally requires an FRPM rate of 40% or higher, which the school meets with a 52.8% rate [1](#ref-1) [4](#ref-4). Specific annual dollar allocations are managed at the district level (New Haven Unified School District) [3](#ref-3).
