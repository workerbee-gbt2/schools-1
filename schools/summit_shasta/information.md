# Summit Public School: Shasta Information

[Back to Schools Overview](../OVERVIEW.md) | [View Contacts](contacts.md)

## School Details
- **Location:** 905 Campus Drive, Daly City, CA [2](#ref-2)
- **Status:** Free public charter high school [1](#ref-1)
- **Founded:** 2013 [1](#ref-1)
- **Grades:** 9-12 [1](#ref-1)
- **Students:** Approximately 449 students [3](#ref-3)

## Mission and Vision
The core vision of Summit Public School: Shasta is that "every student should be equipped to lead a fulfilled life" [4](#ref-4). Its educational approach focuses on developing habits of success, providing 1:1 mentorship, and utilizing project-based learning to prepare students for success in college and life [1](#ref-1).

## Student Demographics
Summit Public School: Shasta has a total minority enrollment of 96% [3](#ref-3). The student body is comprised of the following ethnic groups:
- **Asian:** 56.1% [3](#ref-3)
- **Hispanic:** 27.8% [3](#ref-3)
- **Two or More Races:** 10.7% [3](#ref-3)
- **White:** 3.8% [3](#ref-3)
- **Black:** 0.9% [3](#ref-3)
- **Native Hawaiian/Pacific Islander:** 0.7% [3](#ref-3)

The gender distribution of the student body is 52% male and 48% female [3](#ref-3). Approximately 33% of students are considered economically disadvantaged, with 26% participating in the free lunch program and 7% in the reduced-price lunch program [3](#ref-3).
