## Opportunities & Athletics

### Sports Offered
- **Fall:** Football, Girls Flag Football, Girls Tennis, Volleyball, Cheer, and Boys/Girls Cross Country. [3](#ref-3)
- **Winter:** Boys/Girls Basketball, Boys/Girls Soccer, and Boys/Girls Wrestling. [3](#ref-3)
- **Spring:** Girls Lacrosse, Boys Tennis, Boys/Girls Track & Field, Baseball, Softball, and Swimming. [3](#ref-3)
- **Athletic Administration:** Managed by the [Athletic Directors](contacts.md).

### Athletic Facilities
The school features several key athletic facilities, many of which were part of a major $11.3 million renovation project: [2](#ref-2)
- **Athletic Complex:** Includes a synthetic turf football and soccer field, an eight-lane all-weather track, and new bleachers with a capacity for 1,800 spectators. [2](#ref-2)
- **Gymnasium:** Used for basketball, volleyball, and wrestling. [4](#ref-4)
- **Other Facilities:** The campus includes a cafeteria and a press box associated with the athletic field. [2](#ref-2) [4](#ref-4) Facilities are available for community rental through Facilitron. [4](#ref-4)
