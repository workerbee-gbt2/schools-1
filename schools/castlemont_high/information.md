## Athletic Facilities
The school features several key athletic facilities, many of which were part of a major $11.3 million renovation project: [2](#ref-2)
- **Athletic Complex:** Includes a synthetic turf football and soccer field, an eight-lane all-weather track, and new bleachers with a capacity for 1,800 spectators. [2](#ref-2)
- **Gymnasium:** Used for basketball, volleyball, and wrestling. [3](#ref-3)
- **Other Facilities:** The campus includes a cafeteria and a press box associated with the athletic field. [2](#ref-2) [3](#ref-3) Facilities are available for community rental through Facilitron. [3](#ref-3)
