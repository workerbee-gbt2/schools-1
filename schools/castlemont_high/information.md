# Castlemont High School Information

[Back to Schools Overview](../OVERVIEW.md) | [View Contacts](contacts.md)

## School Details
- **Location:** Oakland, CA [1](#ref-1)
- **Status:** Title 1 [1](#ref-1)
- **Students:** 719 [1](#ref-1)
- **Grades:** 9-12 [1](#ref-1)

## Ratings
- **GreatSchools Rating:** 2/10 [1](#ref-1)
- **Public School Review Rank:** Bottom 50% of California public schools [2](#ref-2)
- **College Readiness Stats:**
    - 4-year graduation rate: 64% (State average: 87%) [1](#ref-1)
    - UC/CSU entrance requirements met: 35% (State average: 53%) [1](#ref-1)
    - SAT 11th grade readiness: 13% (State average: 50%) [1](#ref-1)

## Opportunities & Athletics
- **Career Pathways:** Health, Education, and Visual Storytelling [3](#ref-3)
- **Academic Programs:** 
    - Gifted & Talented program [1](#ref-1)
    - 9th Grade Bridge program [4](#ref-4)
    - Core courses include Ethnic Studies and Urban Ecology [4](#ref-4)
    - Advanced Placement (AP) courses, mentorships, and internships [3](#ref-3)
- **Athletics:** Football, Basketball, Track & Field, and Soccer [6](#ref-6)

## Reviews
- Reviews are mixed. While the school is often perceived externally as one of the 'worst' in Oakland, students have reported it is an 'amazing school' with 'a lot of opportunities' for those who attend [5](#ref-5).
