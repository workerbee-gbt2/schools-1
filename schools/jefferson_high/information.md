# Jefferson High School Information

[Back to Schools Overview](../OVERVIEW.md) | [View Contacts](contacts.md)

## Mission Statement
"Our Mission is to work collectively to meet every student where they are in their learning and to ensure that all students are able to demonstrate mastery of essential content standards upon graduation." [7](#ref-7)

## School Details
- **Location:** 6996 Mission Street, Daly City, CA 94014 [1](#ref-1)
- **Status:** Title 1 [3](#ref-3)
- **Students:** Approximately 1,072 students [1](#ref-1)
- **Demographics:** 44% students from low-income families [1](#ref-1)

## Student Demographics (2023-24) [8](#ref-8)
- **Total Enrollment:** 1,071 students
- **Enrollment by Grade:**
  - Grade 9: 248
  - Grade 10: 295
  - Grade 11: 262
  - Grade 12: 266

- **Enrollment by Student Group:**

  - English Learners: 23.2%

  - Male: 52.8%

## Ratings & Reviews
- **GreatSchools Rating:** 5/10 [1](#ref-1)
- **Test Score Rating:** 5/10 [1](#ref-1)
- **College Readiness Rating:** 4/10 [1](#ref-1)
- **Overall Parent Rating:** 4.7/5 stars based on 6 reviews [2](#ref-2)

## Academics & Programs
- **Academic Programs:** Honors and Advanced Placement (AP) courses [2](#ref-2), Dual Enrollment classes [1](#ref-1).
- **Specialized Programs:** Career Technical Education (CTE) courses, award-winning Robotics program, and Project Lead The Way (STEM) curriculum [1](#ref-1) [2](#ref-2).
- **Extracurricular Activities:** Mission Fusion, Mock Trial, CSF/GATE, PASEO, Filipino Heritage club, Anime, Drama, Art, and Band (marching and concert) [2](#ref-2).
- **Support Services:** After-school support/tutoring programs and supportive counselors [2](#ref-2).

## Athletics
Jefferson High School offers a comprehensive athletics program across three seasons [4](#ref-4):
- **Fall:** Cross Country, Football, Girls Flag Football, Girls Volleyball, Cheer.
- **Winter:** Boys & Girls Basketball, Boys & Girls Soccer, Cheer.
- **Spring:** Badminton, Baseball, Softball, Swimming, Track and Field, Boys Volleyball.

Athletic resources and programs are managed by the [Athletic Directors](contacts.md).

### Facilities
Athletic facilities at the school include [5](#ref-5) [6](#ref-6):
- **Gymnasium:** Used for basketball, volleyball, and physical education [5](#ref-5).
- **JHS Pool:** Supports the aquatics program and swimming team [5](#ref-5) [6](#ref-6).
- **Weight Room:** Equipped for weight training and strength development [6](#ref-6).
- **Track and Field:** Used for track events and physical education [4](#ref-4) [6](#ref-6).
- **TRX Training Area:** Integrated into the fitness curriculum [6](#ref-6).

## Other Info
- The school is recognized for its strong community atmosphere and staff who are deeply invested in student success [2](#ref-2).
- Vigilant safety measures are in place regarding campus environment and events [2](#ref-2).
