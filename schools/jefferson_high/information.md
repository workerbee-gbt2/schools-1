# Jefferson High School Information

## School Details
- **Location:** 6996 Mission Street, Daly City, CA 94014 [1](#ref-1)
- **Status:** Title 1 [2](#ref-2)
- **Students:** Approximately 1,072 students [1](#ref-1)
- **Demographics:** 44% students from low-income families [1](#ref-1)

## Ratings & Reviews
- **GreatSchools Rating:** 5/10 [1](#ref-1)
- **Test Score Rating:** 5/10 [1](#ref-1)
- **College Readiness Rating:** 4/10 [1](#ref-1)
- **Overall Parent Rating:** 4.7/5 stars based on 6 reviews [3](#ref-3)

## Opportunities & Athletics
- **Academic Programs:** Honors and Advanced Placement (AP) courses [3](#ref-3), Dual Enrollment classes [1](#ref-1).
- **Specialized Programs:** Career Technical Education (CTE) courses, award-winning Robotics program, and Project Lead The Way (STEM) curriculum [1](#ref-1) [3](#ref-3).
- **Extracurricular Activities:** Mission Fusion, Mock Trial, CSF/GATE, PASEO, Filipino Heritage club, Anime, Drama, Art, and Band (marching and concert) [3](#ref-3).
- **Support Services:** After-school support/tutoring programs and supportive counselors [3](#ref-3).

## Other Info
- The school is recognized for its strong community atmosphere and staff who are deeply invested in student success [3](#ref-3).
- Vigilant safety measures are in place regarding campus environment and events [3](#ref-3).
