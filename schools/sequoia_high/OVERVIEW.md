# Sequoia High School Overview

Sequoia High School is a public high school in Redwood City, California, known for its International Baccalaureate program and diverse student body.

## Key Information
- **Location:** Redwood City, CA
- **Type:** Public High School
- **Programs:** International Baccalaureate (IB)
