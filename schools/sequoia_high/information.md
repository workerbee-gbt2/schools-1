# Sequoia High School Information

[Back to Schools Overview](../OVERVIEW.md) | [View Contacts](contacts.md)

## School Details
- **Location:** Redwood City, CA [2](#ref-2)
- **Status:** Title 1 School [1](#ref-1), [2](#ref-2)
- **Students:** 1,903 (2023-24 school year) [1](#ref-1)
- **Demographics:** 
    - 34.3% of students are eligible for Free or Reduced Price Meals (FRPM) [1](#ref-1).
    - 45.1% of the student population is classified as socioeconomically disadvantaged [3](#ref-3).

## Funding Status
Sequoia High School is confirmed as a Title 1 school and has been recognized as a Title I Academic Achievement Award School [1](#ref-1), [2](#ref-2).
