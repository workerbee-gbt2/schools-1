## Opportunities & Athletics

### Athletics Program
Sequoia High School offers a wide variety of sports across three seasons [2](#ref-2):
- **Fall:** Boys Water Polo, Cross Country, Football, Girls Flag Football, Girls Golf, Girls Tennis, Girls Volleyball, Girls Water Polo, Sideline Cheer [2](#ref-2).
- **Winter:** Boys Basketball, Boys Soccer, Girls Basketball, Girls Soccer, Sideline Cheer, Wrestling [2](#ref-2).
- **Spring:** Badminton, Baseball, Boys Golf, Boys Lacrosse, Boys Tennis, Boys Volleyball, Girls Lacrosse, Stunt, Softball, Swimming, Track and Field [2](#ref-2).

Additional sports listed on MaxPreps include Baseball, Basketball, Cross Country, Football, Golf, Lacrosse, Soccer, Swimming, Tennis, Track & Field, Volleyball, Water Polo, and Wrestling [1](#ref-1).
