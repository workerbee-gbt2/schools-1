## Opportunities & Athletics

### Athletics Program
Sequoia High School offers a wide variety of sports across three seasons [3](#ref-3):
- **Fall:** Boys Water Polo, Cross Country, Football, Girls Flag Football, Girls Golf, Girls Tennis, Girls Volleyball, Girls Water Polo, Sideline Cheer [3](#ref-3).
- **Winter:** Boys Basketball, Boys Soccer, Girls Basketball, Girls Soccer, Sideline Cheer, Wrestling [3](#ref-3).
- **Spring:** Badminton, Baseball, Boys Golf, Boys Lacrosse, Boys Tennis, Boys Volleyball, Girls Lacrosse, Stunt, Softball, Swimming, Track and Field [3](#ref-3).

Additional sports listed on MaxPreps include Baseball, Basketball, Cross Country, Football, Golf, Lacrosse, Soccer, Swimming, Tennis, Track & Field, Volleyball, Water Polo, and Wrestling [2](#ref-2).

Athletic resources and programs are managed by the [Athletic Department](contacts.md).

### Athletic Facilities
The Sequoia High School campus features several dedicated athletic facilities [5](#ref-5):
- **Gymnasiums:** Gym 1, Gym 2, and Gym 3.
- **Aquatics:** A swimming pool located near Gym 3.
- **Fitness & Training:** A Weight Room and a Dance room.
- **Outdoor Fields:** Baseball/softball field and a stadium/track area.
- **Facility Rentals:** Athletic facilities can be viewed and requested for use via Facilitron [4](#ref-4).
