## CIF Regulations: Sports Physicals
Based on the CIF Constitution and Bylaws (500 Series: General Rules), the following are key regulations for student-athletes:

1. **Annual Requirement**: Student-athletes must undergo a physical examination annually (defined as within the last 365 days).
   - [Source: CIF Bylaw 503.G](https://www.cifstate.org/governance/constitution/500_Series.pdf)
2. **Medical Certification**: The examination must be conducted by a medical practitioner certifying that the student is physically fit for interscholastic athletics.
   - [Source: CIF Bylaw 503.G](https://www.cifstate.org/governance/constitution/500_Series.pdf)
3. **Mandatory Timing**: The physical must be completed **before** any tryout, practice, or competition.
   - [Source: CIF Bylaw 503.G](https://www.cifstate.org/governance/constitution/500_Series.pdf)
4. **Approved Documentation**: Results must be recorded on a form approved by the local school board and include a health history.
   - [Source: CIF Bylaw 503.G](https://www.cifstate.org/governance/constitution/500_Series.pdf)
5. **Recommended Form**: The CIF Sports Medicine Advisory Committee (SMAC) strongly recommends the Pre-Participation Physical Examination (PPE) form endorsed by major medical societies.
   - [Source: CIF Physical Forms](https://www.cifstate.org/sports-medicine/physical_forms/index)
6. **Exemptions**: Exceptions may apply for parent/guardian refusal to consent under specific Education Code provisions.
   - [Source: CIF Bylaw 503.G](https://www.cifstate.org/governance/constitution/500_Series.pdf)
