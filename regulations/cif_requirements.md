# CIF Medical Eligibility Requirements

## Overview
This document covers the California Interscholastic Federation (CIF) requirements for student-athlete physicals, governed by the California Education Code.

## California Education Code Sections

### Physical Examinations
When a school district or county superintendent requires a physical examination for participation in an interscholastic athletic program, the examination may be performed by a physician and surgeon or a physician assistant [1](#ref-1).
*   **Code Section**: EDC Section 49458

### Concussion and Head Injuries
Athletes suspected of sustaining a concussion must be removed from activity and cannot return until evaluated and cleared in writing by a licensed healthcare provider [2](#ref-2).
*   **Code Section**: EDC Section 49475

### Sudden Cardiac Arrest
Under the Eric Paredes Sudden Cardiac Arrest Prevention Act, any pupil who passes out or exhibits symptoms of sudden cardiac arrest during or after athletic activity must be cleared by a physician, nurse practitioner, or physician assistant before returning to play [3](#ref-3).
*   **Code Section**: EDC Section 33479.5
